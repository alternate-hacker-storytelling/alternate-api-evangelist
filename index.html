Alternate API Evangelist

While immersed in a world of tech, reading and writing about real-world issues, I needed an outlet, somewhere I could publish my fictional thoughts and stories. I find it easier to get back to the every day grind of tech blogging, if I have a place to produce fictional work. So I created an alternate reality where I can blog about the weird stuff I think about each day.

In a fake news world it was difficult for me to continue writing on my alternate blogs, but I am beginning to find my way forward, and crave the release that fictional writing brings. I find esign fiction endlessly valuable when it comes to understanding APIs, AI, ML, and the other digital worlds that are merging.

Nothing you read at alternate.apievangelist.com or my other site alternate.kinlane.com is real, if you are looking for my non-fiction work visit Kin Lane or API Evangelist.
